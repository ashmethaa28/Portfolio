About Me

Hey there! I'm Ashmethaa Arulanantham, a driven software engineer passionate about turning ideas into impactful digital solutions. My academic journey led me through the corridors of the University of Guelph, where I majored in Software Engineering and minored in Mathematics, fostering a strong foundation in problem-solving and innovation.

My experience extends into the practical realm of software development, where I've embarked on various projects showcasing my technical prowess. One such project was the creation of a Diabetes Tracker web application, where I leveraged HTML, CSS, TypeScript, Angular.js, and MongoDB to tackle real-world challenges in blood sugar tracking. Before implementation, I meticulously crafted prototypes using Figma, streamlining the development process and ensuring a clear vision for the final product.

I thrive on the thrill of enhancing user experiences. For instance, I revamped a pre-existing open-source social media application, deploying it to the web, refining UI responsiveness, and adding features to amplify its social connectivity. This involved employing MongoDB for efficient data management, and utilizing React, CSS, and JavaScript to bring about UI changes.

Additionally, I dedicated myself to developing the "Goal Diggers Scheduler," a task scheduler mobile app tailored for students. Through Firebase, I facilitated seamless data storage while crafting an intuitive UI using Flutter, empowering users to stay organized effortlessly.

My commitment to growth and staying abreast of industry advancements is evident in my ongoing pursuit of knowledge. Currently, I'm furthering my skill set through a full-stack program on Codecademy, ensuring I stay at the forefront of technological innovations.

I approach every project with meticulous planning, leveraging tools like Figma to create comprehensive prototypes that streamline development and crystallize the vision for the final product.

In essence, my journey in the tech sphere mirrors my passion for innovation, my relentless pursuit of excellence, and my unwavering commitment to continual learning and growth.

Let's connect and explore the boundless possibilities in the dynamic world of technology!

Projects

Memories

During this project, I spearheaded the enhancement of an open-source social media application, bringing it to the web while focusing on improving UI responsiveness and enriching its social interaction features. Implementing MongoDB was instrumental in efficiently storing user and post data, enabling seamless user-post interactions. The redesign of the UI, accomplished through React, CSS, and JavaScript, significantly improved the application's aesthetics and usability. To ensure quality, I designed and implemented both server unit tests and front-end integration tests using Python. Prior to implementation, all design changes were meticulously tested on Figma to guarantee a smooth transition from concept to application.

Diabetes Tracker

In response to real-world challenges faced by individuals managing blood sugar levels, I led the development of a comprehensive Diabetes Tracker web application. Using HTML, CSS, TypeScript, Angular.js, and MongoDB, we created a robust platform for tracking and managing blood sugar data. Before implementation, a thorough prototype was designed on Figma, streamlining the development process and ensuring a clear direction for the final product. Efficient task coordination and problem-solving skills played a pivotal role in overcoming challenges and delivering a timely and impactful solution to aid individuals dealing with diabetes.

Goal Digger

Developing a task scheduler mobile app tailored for students was the primary focus during this project. Leveraging Firebase, I efficiently stored task-related information, ensuring accessibility and reliability for users. The app's user interface was crafted using Flutter, providing an intuitive and engaging experience. By combining functionality with an appealing design, the app aimed to assist students in organizing their schedules effectively, enhancing their productivity and time management skills.
